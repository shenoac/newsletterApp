KeelWorks Diversity

Diversity is one of the five Keelworks values. Fostering inclusion & community, each month we mark the events that are culturally important to our global community.

February 12: Lantern Festival

A traditional Chinese festival that signifies the conclusion of Lunar New Year celebration. In this festival, we float lanterns, dance the lion dance, and to bring good fortune, we solve riddles written on the lanterns.

February 14: Valentine's Day

Saint Valentine married couples in defiance of the emperor Claudius II. For this, he is associated with romantic love and his feast day is marked by cards, gifts, and flowers to the ones we love.

February 15: Maha Shivaratri

A sacred Hindu festival dedicated to Lord Shiva, observed through fasting, prayers, and night-long vigils as devotees seek spiritual growth and divine blessings.

February 21: International Mother Language Day

Honoring the 1952 Language Movement in Bangladesh, this observance serves as a poignant reminder that language transcends mere words—it embodies identity, heritage, and the voice of people.

February 28: Ramadan Begins

The holy month of Ramadan is a time for fasting, prayer, and reflection, celebrated by Muslims around the globe, beginning with the sighting of the new moon.

Product Highlight

ISD Process App

1- Objective
This product manages instructional design processes and contains all process documents increasing process consistency. The application supports coarse ideation with problem data, project assignment, and needs analysis through to storyboarding. It automatically loops in quality assurance before any component is sent to stakeholder review. Requiring problem data at the start supports objective measurement holding learning accountable to measured change.

2- Expected Timeline & Progress
This product has been a year in the making and the first version is expected by the end of first quarter 2025.

3- How application helps keelworks:
This application supports course process efficiency, course accountability, and better course design. There is nothing like it on the market. If we are right about its benefits, we should be able to market it to raise funds for the foundation.
